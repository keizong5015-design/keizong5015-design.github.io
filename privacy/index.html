Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

DadIn Auto Publisher ("we", "us", or "our") operates an affiliate marketing automation service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service and its TikTok integration.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize our Service through TikTok Login Kit, we collect:

  • TikTok Open ID: A unique identifier for your TikTok account
  • Display Name: Your TikTok display name
  • Access Token: A temporary token used to publish content on your behalf

We do not collect passwords, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal information from TikTok beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and verify your TikTok account
  • Publish affiliate marketing videos to your TikTok account via the Content Posting API
  • Display your account information within our application dashboard

3. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens obtained through TikTok OAuth are stored only in your browser session and are not persisted to any external database or server. When your session ends, the token is discarded. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information during transmission.

4. TikTok API Scopes Used

Our application requests the following TikTok permissions:

  • user.info.basic: Read your TikTok Open ID and display name
  • video.upload: Upload and publish video content to your TikTok account

5. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with the following third-party platforms:

  • TikTok: For user authentication and video publishing
  • Pexels: For royalty-free video stock footage
  • Shopee, AliExpress, Amazon: For affiliate product data

Each of these services has its own Privacy Policy governing their data practices.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We do not share your TikTok account data with any third parties except as required by law or as necessary to provide the Service.

7. Data Retention

We retain session data only for the duration of your active session. No personal data is stored permanently on our servers beyond basic application logs required for security and debugging purposes, which are automatically deleted after 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke TikTok access authorization at any time through TikTok account settings
  • Request information about what data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you

To exercise these rights, contact us at keizong5015@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy
